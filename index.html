Coronavirus (SARS-COV-2)

What is it?

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by coronavirus. This virus and disease was unknown before the outbreak began in Wuhan, China, in December 2019. COVID-19 became a pandemic affecting many countries globally. Most people who fall sick with COVID-19 will experience mild to moderate symptoms and recover without special treatment.
